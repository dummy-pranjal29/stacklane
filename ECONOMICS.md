**Converted lead value**:
A closed customer for Credex typically spends $50,000/year on AI infrastructure. Assuming a conservative 15% margin on resold credits, a single converted customer yields $7,500 in annual gross profit.

**CAC per channel**:

- Manual outbound (Cold DMs/LinkedIn): ~$0 CAC, only time investment.
- Community Content (HackerNews/Reddit): $0 CAC.
- Paid Ads (If scaled): Target CAC of <$500 per qualified consultation.

**Conversion funnel**:

- 500 Audits run per month
- 10% capture rate → 50 qualified leads captured
- 20% consultation booking rate → 10 consultations held
- 10% close rate → 1 net new customer per month

**Profitability threshold**:
Because the application costs less than $50/month to host (Vercel + MongoDB Atlas + Groq API), the break-even point is functionally zero. Closing just one customer ($7,500 profit) pays for years of runway and development time.

**$1M ARR in 18 months**:
To hit $1M in ARR solely from leads generated by this tool, we need ~133 customers (at $7,500 profit). To achieve this in 18 months, we need to close 7.3 customers per month. Working backwards through the funnel: we need 73 consultations → 365 leads → 3,650 audits per month (approx. 120 audits per day). This requires transitioning from manual outbound to programmatic SEO scaling and affiliate partnerships.
