## FAQ (5 Q&As)

### Q: Is this completely free?

**A:** Yes. We build this tool to help founders optimize AI spending and identify opportunities for Credex discounted credits. No signup required to use the audit.

### Q: How accurate are the recommendations?

**A:** Very accurate for plan downgrades (easy math). For cross-tool substitutions, we compare documented capabilities. All pricing is sourced from official vendor pages, updated weekly.

### Q: Will you share my data?

**A:** No. Audits are private by default. If you email-gate your results or choose to share, you control what information is visible.

### Q: What if I don't save that much?

**A:** We're honest. If you're already on the right plan for your team size, we'll tell you so. Not every team overspends, and that's okay.

### Q: What happens after the audit?

**A:** For teams identifying >$500/month in savings, Credex offers discounted credits on Claude, ChatGPT, and other tools—typically 30–40% below retail. You'll be notified if you're eligible. No obligation.

## Voice & Tone

- **Authoritative, not salesy**: We're consultants, not marketing
- **Specific, not vague**: "Save $X/month on Cursor" not "Incredible savings!"
- **Founder-to-founder**: Speak like peers; assume engineers read the page
- **Numbers-first**: Finance leaders want data, not stories
- **Honest about limits**: "We're great at plan fit; worse at predicting your future usage"
